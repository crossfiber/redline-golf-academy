Frequently Asked Questions About Golf Training in Fort Myers

Each 60-minute session is structured as 40 minutes of expert swing coaching combined with 20 minutes of golf-specific strength training. This integrated approach addresses both technique and physical capability, ensuring you're improving across all areas that impact performance.

Our training is designed for golfers at all skill levels, from beginners working on fundamentals to experienced golfers looking to maximize distance and consistency. We customize the coaching and strength work to match your current level and goals.

Bring your own clubs if you prefer working with them, though we have a range of equipment available. Wear comfortable athletic clothing that allows full range of motion. The strength training portion requires athletic shoes. All other training equipment is provided at the facility.

Traditional golf lessons focus on swing mechanics alone. Elite Golf Performance Training integrates swing coaching with golf-specific strength and mobility training. We address both technique and physical capability, recognizing that your body's limitations directly impact your ability to execute the swing we're coaching.
