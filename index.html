Distance Training Backed by PGA Tour Data

We know what works because we base our programs on PGA Tour performance data. The average PGA Tour driving distance is 300 yards, while top 10 drivers achieve 315 yards or more.

The mechanics are clear: plus 1 mph of clubhead speed equals 2 to 3 yards of carry distance. Plus 1 mph of ball speed equals 2 yards of carry distance.

Our structured programs consistently yield clubhead speed gains of 5 to 10 mph, translating to 10 to 20 plus yards of additional carry distance.
