What a session looks like

Numbers That Show It.

Every session at Redline runs the same loop: measure where you are, fix what's losing speed, build the body that holds the fix, then prove it on the launch monitor before you walk out.

01

Baseline

Launch monitor captures clubhead speed, ball speed, smash factor, and carry so we know exactly where you start.

02

Swing Work

Forty minutes of hands-on coaching with 3D analysis. We fix the specific mechanics that are costing you distance.

03

Strength

Twenty minutes of golf-specific work: hip rotation, thoracic mobility, lead-leg stability, anti-rotation core.

04

Retest

Back on the monitor. Compare numbers before vs. after. Most golfers see 2 to 4 mph of gain in a single session.

Every drill on the floor exists because the launch monitor said so. No filler. No tip-of-the-week. The data dictates the program.

Why both, every time

Perfect Mechanics
Won't Save You
If The Body Quits.

You can have a textbook swing and still leave distance on the table. If your hips can't rotate fast enough or your core can't stabilize at impact, the swing breaks down under pressure. That gap is what most coaches ignore.

Rotational power + swing efficiency = speed that holds up on the course.

Redline trains both sides in every session. Your swing gets more efficient AND you build the physical capacity to execute it at full speed, shot after shot. That's how golfers add 5 to 10 mph and actually keep it.
